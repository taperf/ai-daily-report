## 🎙️ Podcast: Biohub — The Future of Biology is Open-Source

**No Priors** with Mark Zuckerberg, Priscilla Chan & Alex Rives

扎克伯格和陈慧琳谈Biohub虚拟生物学计划——用AI理解生物系统。

Key insights:
- **$500M committed** to virtual biology initiative — building frontier AI models for biology
- "Unlike language models where data is all over the internet, biology data needs to be **invented through novel scientific approaches**"
- Already folded over **1.1 billion protein structures** — the model understands proteins generically, not designed for any specific target
- Zuckerberg: "End of century" disease cure timeline now feels "too conservative"
- Core thesis: couple a frontier AI lab with a frontier biology lab, because you need both the models AND the data generation capability
- Open-source approach: "We'll have a bigger impact by getting it in more scientists' hands quicker"

核心洞察：生物学AI不像语言模型有海量互联网数据，需要发明全新的科学方法来生成数据。已折叠超11亿蛋白质结构。扎克伯格认为「本世纪末治愈所有疾病」的时间线现在看来「太保守了」。

🔗 https://www.youtube.com/@NoPriorsPodcast
