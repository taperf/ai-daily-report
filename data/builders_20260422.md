**No Priors: "The Agentic Economy: How AI Agents Will Transform the Financial System" with Circle CEO Jeremy Allaire**

**The Takeaway:** The financial infrastructure needed for an AI-driven economy doesn't exist yet — and stablecoins + blockchain operating systems are the most viable path to building it.

Circle co-founder and CEO Jeremy Allaire makes a compelling case that the emerging "agentic economy" — where AI agents conduct work, collaborate, and purchase services from each other — requires fundamentally new financial infrastructure. Today's banking system can't support globally interoperable, instant, programmable micro-transactions at scale. USDC, now backed by law through the Genius Act, operates as "full reserve money" backed by short-duration T-bills (average 13-day duration), and is already being used by everything from game micropayments to multi-hundred-million-dollar capital markets settlements.

The most forward-looking part of the conversation: Allaire envisions on-chain organizations with "a mixture of human and agentic actors" that could become "the most productive corporate forms we've ever seen in economic history." He's also intrigued by the concept of productive proof-of-work — using AI inference compute as the basis for cryptocurrency mining, where "the work itself is the inference." On GDP, he thinks "double digit GDP numbers in the 2030s seems not unrealistic," but cautions that "the risk here is that GDP growth is capital capturing more capital at the expense of humans."

Circle is launching **ARC**, which Allaire describes as "an economic operating system" — a purpose-built blockchain with USDC as native token, known validator sets (major financial infrastructure companies), deterministic settlement finality in hundreds of milliseconds, transactions at "a millionth of a penny," and built-in privacy primitives.

Circle 联合创始人兼 CEO Jeremy Allaire 提出了一个有说服力的论点：正在涌现的"Agent 经济"——AI Agent 执行工作、彼此协作、互相购买服务——需要全新的金融基础设施。现有银行体系无法支持全球互通的、即时的、可编程的大规模微交易。USDC 现已通过 Genius Act 获得法律支持，作为"全额准备金货币"由短期国债支撑（平均 13 天期限），从游戏微支付到数亿美元的资本市场结算都在使用。
