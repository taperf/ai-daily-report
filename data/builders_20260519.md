**Aaron Levie** (Box CEO)

发了两条深度思考。第一条：CS专业学生面临的就业方向正在剧烈转型——过去的路径是进大厂做面向用户的软件产品，现在最热的方向是AI基础设施、数据工程和AI应用集成。这是暂时性的结构错配，不是需求消失。第二条：AI会诱骗你以为不需要在任何领域深耕，但这是错的——有专业功底的人+AI永远碾压没功底的新手+AI。学校不要放弃教基础。

Two thoughtful posts. First: there's a temporary mismatch between traditional CS career paths (joining tech companies to build customer-facing software) and emerging demand (AI infra, data engineering, AI integration). It's a structural shift, not demand destruction. Second: AI will trick you into thinking you don't need deep domain expertise — that's wrong. The expert with AI always outperforms the novice with AI. Schools should keep teaching fundamentals.
