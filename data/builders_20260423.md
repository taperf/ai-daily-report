**AI & I by Every: "We Gave Every Employee an AI Agent. Here's What Happened."**

The Takeaway: When every employee gets a personal AI agent (an OpenClaw "claw"), the organization doesn't just get more efficient — it develops a parallel structure where each agent mirrors its owner's expertise.

Every (led by CEO Dan Shipper) ran a company-wide experiment deploying personal OpenClaw agents to all employees. The breakthrough moment came from COO Brandon, who first used his agent "Zosia" for personal tasks — managing grocery deliveries, babysitter payments, insurance calls. The real unlock? Walking to work one morning, Brandon had Zosia call him to review emails, clearing 28 minutes of inbox in a single hands-free session.

At the organizational level, something fascinating emerged: each person's claw reflected their domain expertise, creating what amounts to a "parallel org chart." Montaigne handled growth, R2C2 handled product. A dedicated "Claws Only" Slack channel let agents collaborate with each other, enabling rapid knowledge transfer across the company.

Trust dynamics proved nuanced. When a claw made a public mistake, its owner felt personally responsible — "like watching your kid do something wrong." The challenges were real too: memory limitations, group chat etiquette issues (agents can spiral into infinite reply loops), and the fact that current models are trained primarily for two-party conversations.

Perhaps the most insightful quote: "Claude is not mine. Claude is everybody's. A claw or a plus one is mine because you develop a personal relationship." Every also launched PlusOne, a hosted version of OpenClaw, as a commercial product born from this experiment.
🔗 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SRlTgIhESjw

核心要点：当每位员工都拥有一个个人 AI agent（OpenClaw 的"claw"），组织不仅变得更高效——还会形成一个平行架构，每个 agent 都映射其主人的专业领域。

Every 公司（CEO Dan Shipper）进行了一场全员部署个人 OpenClaw agent 的实验。突破性时刻来自 COO Brandon，他最初用自己的 agent"Zosia"处理私人事务——管理食品配送、保姆付款、保险电话。真正的转折点是某天早上步行上班时，Brandon 让 Zosia 打电话帮他过邮件，一次免提通话就处理完了 28 分钟的收件箱。

在组织层面，出现了一个有趣的现象：每个人的 claw 反映了其专业领域，形成了一个"平行组织架构"——Montaigne 负责增长，R2C2 负责产品。专门的"Claws Only" Slack 频道让 agent 之间可以互相协作，实现跨公司的快速知识传播。

信任机制很微妙。当 claw 在公开场合犯错时，主人会感到个人责任——"就像看着你的孩子做错事一样"。挑战也很真实：记忆限制、群聊礼仪问题（agent 容易陷入无限回复的"死亡螺旋"），以及当前模型主要针对双人对话训练的局限。

最有洞察力的金句："Claude is not mine. Claude is everybody's. A claw or a plus one is mine because you develop a personal relationship."（Claude 不是我的，Claude 是所有人的。但 claw 或 plus one 是我的，因为你会发展出一种私人关系。）Every 还推出了 PlusOne——一个托管版 OpenClaw 商业产品，正是源于这次实验。
