## 🎙️ PODCAST

### Training Data: Andrej Karpathy — From Vibe Coding to Agentic Engineering

**The Takeaway:** We are entering "Software 3.0" — a paradigm where neural networks are the host process and traditional code becomes the coprocessor, and the implications are far more radical than just "coding faster."

Karpathy lays out a vivid framework: Software 1.0 = explicit code, Software 2.0 = learned weights, Software 3.0 = prompting an LLM as a programmable computer. His key insight: this isn't just about speedups — entire categories of apps (like his own MenuGen) *shouldn't exist* in the new paradigm. The "Software 3.0 version" is just giving a photo and a prompt to a model that outputs the finished result directly.

On **verifiability**: LLMs progress fastest where outputs can be verified (math, code), creating "jagged intelligence" — the same model that refactors 100K-line codebases will tell you to *walk* to a car wash 50 meters away. Founders should look for valuable RL environments the labs *aren't* focusing on.

On **agentic engineering**: December 2025 was the "stark transition" — when chunks of generated code started just *working* without correction. Karpathy describes never feeling more behind as a programmer, but reframes it: the new programming paradigm is "what's the piece of text to copy-paste to your agent?"

His most provocative claim: you could imagine "completely neural computers" where neural nets do the heavy lifting and CPUs become mere coprocessors — we're heading toward the neural net as the host process.

**播客精华：Andrej Karpathy — 从 Vibe Coding 到智能体工程**

**核心观点**：我们正进入「软件3.0」范式——神经网络成为主进程，传统代码退化为协处理器。这不仅仅是「编码加速」，而是根本性的计算范式转移。

Karpathy提出清晰框架：软件1.0=显式代码，软件2.0=训练权重，软件3.0=将LLM当作可编程计算机来提示。他用自己的MenuGen应用举例——在新范式下这类应用*根本不该存在*，直接一张照片+一句提示词就能得到最终结果。

关于**可验证性**：LLM在输出可验证的领域进步最快（数学、代码），形成「锯齿状智能」——同一个模型能重构十万行代码库，却会建议你步行去50米外的洗车店。创始人应该寻找实验室*尚未聚焦*的有价值RL环境。

关于**智能体工程**：2025年12月是「剧烈转折点」——生成的代码开始无需修改直接运行。Karpathy说他从未觉得自己作为程序员如此落后，但重新定义了编程范式：「现在的编程就是——给你的Agent复制粘贴什么文本？」

## 📊 Today's Themes | 今日主题

**1. Enterprise AI Deployment is the new mega-market（企业AI部署成为超级赛道）**
From Levie's prediction about deployment work exceeding imagination, to OpenAI/Anthropic both launching JV deployment companies with Wall Street backers today — the consensus is forming: model quality is table stakes, distribution + implementation is the game.

**2. Personal sovereignty over AI（AI个人主权）**
Garry Tan's manifesto + Karpathy's "what text do you paste to your agent" framing both point the same direction: the future belongs to individuals who own their prompts, data, and agent infrastructure — not those locked into platforms.

**3. The coding agent inflection is real（编程Agent拐点已至）**
Karpathy's December transition, Masad's 700+ completed projects, Zara's "just you and an agent" — multiple signals from different vantage points confirming that autonomous code generation has crossed a quality threshold.
