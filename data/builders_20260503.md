**Anthropic Engineering: "An update on recent Claude Code quality reports"**

Anthropic published a detailed postmortem on why Claude Code felt degraded for some users over the past month. Three separate issues were identified and fixed:

1. **Reasoning effort downgrade (Mar 4)**: Default was changed from high to medium to reduce latency. Users noticed worse quality. Reverted April 7.
2. **Caching bug (Mar 26)**: A bug meant to clear stale thinking once per session instead cleared it every turn, making Claude "forgetful." Fixed April 10.
3. **Verbosity prompt (Apr 16)**: A system prompt adding "keep text between tool calls to ≤25 words" hurt coding quality. Reverted April 20.

Key quote: "We never intentionally degrade our models." Anthropic is resetting usage limits for all subscribers and improving internal testing processes.

Anthropic发布了一篇详细的事后分析，解释了为什么过去一个月部分用户感觉Claude Code质量下降。确认了三个独立问题并已修复：

1. **推理effort降级（3月4日）**：默认从high改为medium以减少延迟，用户反映质量变差，4月7日回滚。
2. **缓存bug（3月26日）**：本应只清除一次过期思考内容的功能，变成了每轮都清除，让Claude变得「健忘」。4月10日修复。
3. **简洁性prompt（4月16日）**：系统prompt中添加「工具调用间文本≤25词」的限制损害了编码质量。4月20日撤回。

关键引用：「我们从未故意降低模型质量。」Anthropic为所有订阅者重置了使用限额，并改进了内部测试流程。

**No Priors: "Baseten CEO Tuhin Srivastava on the AI Inference Crunch, Custom Models, and Building the Inference Cloud"**

The Takeaway: AI inference is "the last market" — even if AGI arrives, all that's left is inference. And we're nowhere near meeting demand.

Baseten has grown 30x in 12 months and expects to surpass $1B in revenue this year. CEO Tuhin Srivastava paints a picture of extreme supply constraint: "There is very, very little slack compute available... we run at mid-nineties utilization most of the time." The company operates 90 clusters across 18 clouds globally.

Most surprising insight: 95% of tokens served on Baseten are from custom models — not vanilla open source. Every customer is modifying weights for their specific use case. "No one is just running the vanilla open source weights." This validates the post-training thesis: the real value isn't in base models, it's in specialized inference.

On the chip landscape, Srivastava was diplomatic but clear: "People really underestimate NVIDIA's supply chain and CUDA ecosystem. You can move fastest with NVIDIA today." Multi-chip future is desirable but years away from being competitive.

On enterprise AI adoption: "If you look by inference count, it'd be 99% in the future. The majority of the market hasn't come online yet." Enterprise is still early innings despite all the hype.

核心观点：AI推理是「最后的市场」——即便AGI到来，剩下的也全是推理。而我们离满足需求还差得远。

Baseten在12个月内增长了30倍，预计今年收入将超过10亿美元。CEO Tuhin Srivastava描绘了极端的供给约束：「可用的闲置算力非常非常少……我们大部分时间运行在95%的利用率。」公司在全球18个云上运营90个集群。

最令人惊讶的洞察：Baseten上服务的95%的token来自定制模型，而非原版开源模型。每个客户都在为自己的场景修改权重。「没有人在跑原版开源权重。」这验证了后训练论点：真正的价值不在基础模型，而在专业化推理。

关于企业AI采用：「如果按推理量算，99%还在未来。市场的大部分还没上线。」尽管有各种炒作，企业AI仍处于早期阶段。
