## OFFICIAL BLOGS

**Anthropic Engineering：Scaling Managed Agents: Decoupling the brain from the hands**

Anthropic发布了Managed Agents的架构深度解析。核心思路是将agent的「大脑」（Claude及其harness）与「双手」（sandbox和工具）以及「记忆」（session日志）解耦。

关键设计：
- Session作为持久化事件日志存在于context window之外，通过getEvents()接口让harness按需检索上下文
- Sandbox变成「cattle」（可替换）而非「pet」（不可失去）——容器挂了就重起新的，harness捕获错误传给Claude决定是否重试
- 安全边界：凭证永远不进入sandbox，通过MCP proxy和vault隔离
- 性能收益：解耦后p50 TTFT下降约60%，p95下降超过90%
- 支持多brain多hand架构：一个brain可以操作多个sandbox，brain之间可以传递hand

本质是将OS虚拟化硬件的思路应用到agent架构——接口稳定，实现可换。

Anthropic published a deep dive on Managed Agents architecture. Core idea: decouple the "brain" (Claude + harness) from the "hands" (sandboxes/tools) and the "session" (event log). Key results: p50 TTFT dropped ~60%, p95 dropped over 90%. Security boundary ensures credentials never enter the sandbox. Supports many-brains-many-hands topology.

🔗 https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/managed-agents

**Training Data：Rebuilding IT From the Ground Up for the AI Age: Serval's Jake Stauch**

核心观点：Serval的创始人Jake Stauch认为，如果你想推动企业自动化，构建自动化的过程必须比被自动化的工作流程本身更简单。Serval保留了ServiceNow的核心抽象（workflow on top of database），但用AI来构建workflow和更新database，实现即时自动化。

关键洞察：
- 产品护城河不是能力而是边界——「The product is the boundaries. The product is the controls.」大企业需要权限、审批、审计、日志来舒适地部署AI
- 双agent架构：admin agent构建工具和技能（带审批权限），helpdesk agent面向终端用户（只能用admin明确批准的工具）
- Solutions Engineering完全被AI替代——AE通过Serval实时获取产品答案，不再需要SE
- 最大挑战仍然是招人——「people remain the biggest moat you can have」
- 企业中个人想要自主权 vs 组织想要控制的张力，是当前AI部署的核心矛盾

The Takeaway: If you want to drive enterprise automation, making the automation process simpler than the manual task is the key insight. Serval keeps ServiceNow's primitives (workflows on databases) but uses AI to build them instantly. Key quote: "The product is the boundaries. The product is the controls." Their dual-agent architecture (admin builds tools with approvals, helpdesk agent uses only approved tools) solves enterprise AI's core tension between autonomy and control.
