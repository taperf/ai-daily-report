🎙️ Podcast Spotlight

Training Data: From SEO to Agent-Led Growth
嘉宾 Guest: James Cadwallader, Profound CEO

The age of marketing to humans is ending. James Cadwallader argues that AI agents have replaced consumers as the primary "users" of the internet. "It's not that the front door of the Internet has changed. The person going through the door has changed — from a consumer using blue links to an agent discovering your brand, then coming back to maintain the relationship with the consumer."

Key insights: When he searched for a showerhead on ChatGPT, it used 65 different web pages to form its answer — orders of magnitude wider than any human would browse. Marketers must now create content designed to be discovered AND consumed entirely by agents. The old SEO playbook of ranking in top 5 blue links is dead; agents use the long tail aggressively.

His boldest prediction: dead internet theory could materialize within 3 years. As humans stop clicking into websites, advertising-funded publishing collapses, and the incentive to create original content drops to zero. His solution? AI labs will vertically integrate with social media networks (think OpenAI acquiring Reddit) — because social platforms are the last bastions of authentic human data. "50% of the web is now utilizing AI-written content," and comparative listicles designed to game AI recommendations are "working very effectively" right now.

On AI-era advertising: "Generative advertising in a conversational interface with higher levels of personalization will be the most effective form of advertising the world has ever seen."

🔗 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RyTwRCKeDo4

营销面向人类的时代正在终结。James Cadwallader认为，AI agent已经取代消费者成为互联网的主要"用户"。"不是互联网的大门变了，而是走进大门的人变了——从使用蓝色链接的消费者，变成了发现你的品牌、再回来维系与消费者关系的agent。"

核心洞察：当他在ChatGPT上搜索花洒时，AI用了65个不同的网页来生成答案——比任何人类浏览的范围都广出几个数量级。营销人员现在必须创建完全为agent发现和消费而设计的内容。旧的SEO策略已死；agent会积极利用互联网的长尾内容。

最大胆的预测："死亡互联网"理论可能在3年内成为现实。当人类不再点击网站，广告驱动的出版业崩塌，创造原创内容的动力归零。他的答案是：AI实验室将与社交媒体网络垂直整合（比如OpenAI收购Reddit）——因为社交平台是真实人类数据的最后堡垒。"50%的网页已经使用AI生成内容"，而用来操纵AI推荐的对比测评文章目前"效果非常好"。

关于AI时代的广告："在对话界面中实现高度个性化的生成式广告，将是人类历史上最有效的广告形式。"
