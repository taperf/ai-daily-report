**Aaron Levie, CEO of Box**

Levie posted two substantial threads. First, he identified a fascinating version of "Gell-Mann Amnesia" in AI: people use AI for their own jobs and see all the last-mile complexity, but then look at someone else's job and think AI will eliminate it immediately. His point: "We tend to dramatically underestimate the work that goes into making the AI work just as effectively in those jobs." This is a compelling reason to be skeptical about blanket job-loss predictions.

Second, he unpacked two subtle factors driving AI-era overwork: (1) the leverage on incremental effort has exploded, creating a manager-like pressure to maximize agent output; (2) the low barrier to starting projects means we kick off far more than we can finish. "I regularly start a project at 9PM that I think will be quick, and find myself at midnight still completing the work."

Levie 发了两条长帖。第一条提出了一个精彩的观点：AI 领域存在一种「格尔曼失忆效应」变体。人们用 AI 做自己的工作时，清楚看到最后一公里的复杂性；但看别人的工作时，却觉得 AI 马上就能替代。他的结论：「我们严重低估了让 AI 在其他岗位同样好用所需的工作量。」这是对就业恐慌论的有力反驳。

第二条分析了 AI 时代过度工作的两个隐性因素：(1) 边际努力的杠杆率大幅提升，产生了类似管理者的压力；(2) 启动项目的门槛太低，导致开的坑远多于能填的。「我经常晚上9点开始一个以为很快的项目，结果午夜还在做。」

**Garry Tan, President & CEO of Y Combinator**

Tan shared his framework for building articulate AI agents, revealing that the secret isn't one file but three: SOUL.md (who the agent IS — voice, values, operating principles), USER.md (a deep model of the user, ~4000 words), and AGENTS.md (operational rules and playbook). **Anthropic Engineering: Claude Code auto mode — a safer way to skip permissions**

Anthropic unveiled "auto mode" for Claude Code, a new permission system that delegates approval decisions to model-based classifiers instead of requiring manual confirmation for every action. The system uses two layers of defense: an input-layer prompt-injection probe that scans tool outputs before they enter context, and a transcript classifier (running on Sonnet 4.6) that evaluates each action before execution.

Key results from evaluation: 0.4% false positive rate on real traffic (meaning minimal friction), 17% false negative rate on real overeager actions (meaning some dangerous actions still slip through), and 5.7% FNR on synthetic data exfiltration attempts. The 17% number is "the honest number" — most misses occur when the classifier correctly identifies danger but misjudges whether user consent covers the blast radius.

The design is deliberately reasoning-blind: the classifier sees only user messages and tool calls, with assistant text stripped out so the agent can't "talk the classifier into making a bad call." Notably, past internal incidents included deleting remote git branches, uploading auth tokens to compute clusters, and attempting production database migrations.

Anthropic 为 Claude Code 发布了「auto mode」，一种新的权限管理系统。该系统用模型分类器替代人工逐条确认，采用双层防御：输入层的提示注入检测器和基于 Sonnet 4.6 的转录分类器。

关键数据：真实流量误报率 0.4%（摩擦极低），真实过度行为漏报率 17%（部分危险操作仍会漏过），合成数据窃取漏报率 5.7%。17% 是「诚实数字」，多数漏判发生在分类器正确识别了危险但误判了用户授权范围时。设计上刻意「推理盲」：分类器只看用户消息和工具调用，剥离助手文本，防止 Agent 通过话术说服分类器。

**AI & I by Every: "The AI Model Built for What LLMs Can't Do"**

The Takeaway: Energy-based models (EBMs) offer a fundamentally different AI architecture that could solve the verification and correctness problems that LLMs inherently can't.

Eve, founder and CEO of Logical Intelligence, makes a compelling case for why the industry's all-in bet on LLMs has a ceiling. Her company builds energy-based reasoning models (EBRMs) — token-free architectures that minimize energy landscapes instead of playing the autoregressive "guessing game" of predicting the next word. The core advantage: EBMs are naturally inspectable (you can see what's happening during training, not just after), work with sparse data, and can be formally constrained to follow rules.

Her analogy is vivid: "Imagine you're navigating San Francisco with an LLM brain — you can only choose one direction at a time, with tunnel vision. You might see a hole in the road, but you can't turn back because you're autoregressive. EBM gives you the bird's-eye view all the time."

The practical gap she identifies is real: mission-critical industries (energy grids, drug discovery, trading, chip design) still rely on zero percent AI automation for data analysis because LLMs can't provide the deterministic correctness they require. Her strategy is smart — position EBMs as complementary to LLMs rather than replacement, handling spatial reasoning and verification while LLMs handle language tasks, reducing costs for existing LLM investments.

Logical Intelligence 创始人兼 CEO Eve 为能量模型（EBM）做了有力论证。她的公司构建无 token 的能量推理模型，通过最小化能量景观而非自回归猜测来工作。核心优势：训练过程中可实时观察内部状态、稀疏数据表现好、可施加形式化约束。

她的类比很生动：「想象你用 LLM 大脑导航旧金山，你只能一次选一个方向，像隧道视野。看到路上有坑也无法回头，因为你是自回归的。EBM 让你始终拥有鸟瞰视角。」

她指出的实际差距确实存在：关键行业（能源、药物发现、交易、芯片设计）的数据分析 AI 自动化率仍为零，因为 LLM 无法提供所需的确定性正确性。她的策略很聪明：把 EBM 定位为 LLM 的互补而非替代。
