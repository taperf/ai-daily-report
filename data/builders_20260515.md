## 🎙️ Podcast

### Training Data: Suno's Mikey Shulman — "Everyone Can Make Music Now"
Published: May 13, 2026

**Key takeaways from Suno's CEO:**

**1. Music AI ≠ Scale problem.** Unlike LLMs where bigger = better, Suno's models are relatively small. Music has no benchmarks and no "right answers," so scale is less helpful. The breakthroughs come from research + massive user preference data, not from brute-force compute.

**2. 90% of Suno users create something every day.** This is the key insight: people aren't creating music to distribute it elsewhere — creation itself is the entertainment. Suno calls this "creative entertainment," analogous to why people enjoy cooking even when restaurants exist.

**3. The model doesn't know music theory.** Suno deliberately doesn't teach the model about 12 tones or 200 instruments. By modeling pure sound waves (sampled 48,000 times/second), the model isn't constrained — users get microtonal music, genre blends (trap + sitar, country + 808s) that traditional tools would never produce.

**4. Warner partnership = building things that couldn't exist before.** Not "AI Spotify," but interactive experiences where fans co-create with artists. Mikey hopes for truly interactive concerts within 12 months where audiences make music together with performers.

**5. $300M revenue run rate, but "it's really early."** Most people still don't know about Suno. The product focus is making it more social (co-creation, sharing templates) and more personal (using your own voice in songs).

**6. On "slop":** "I made two songs with my five-year-old yesterday. Is that slop? Sure, 99.999% of the planet has no interest. But it's meaningful to me." When laptops democratized beat-making, people said the same thing — and that era produced entirely new genres and stars.

---

**Suno CEO关于AI音乐的核心观点：**

1. **音乐AI不是规模问题**：不同于LLM越大越好，Suno的模型相对较小。音乐没有benchmark，没有标准答案，研究突破+海量用户偏好数据才是关键。

2. **90%用户每天都在创作**：人们不是为了分发音乐而创作——创作本身就是娱乐。这叫「创意娱乐」，就像人们享受烹饪即使餐厅的菜更好。

3. **模型不懂乐理**：Suno刻意不教模型12音阶或200种乐器的概念。通过直接建模原始声波（每秒采样48000次），模型不受约束，用户能得到传统工具永远无法产出的微分音、跨界混搭。

4. **与华纳合作 = 创造前所未有的体验**：不是做AI版Spotify，而是让粉丝与艺术家互动共创。Mikey希望12个月内实现观众参与式互动演唱会。

5. **年化收入3亿美元，但「还非常早期」**：产品方向是更社交（共创、模板分享）和更个人化（在歌曲中使用自己的声音）。

6. **关于「AI垃圾」（slop）**：当笔记本电脑让更多人能做beat时，人们也说过同样的话——结果催生了全新的音乐流派和新星。

### Claude Blog: New in Claude Managed Agents — Dreaming, Outcomes, and Multiagent Orchestration
Published: May 6, 2026

Anthropic launched three major updates to Claude Managed Agents:

**1. Dreaming (Research Preview)** — A scheduled process that reviews agent sessions and memory, extracts patterns, and curates memories for self-improvement. It surfaces recurring mistakes, converging workflows, and shared team preferences. Can update memory automatically or with human review. Designed for long-running work and multiagent setups.

**2. Outcomes** — Define a success rubric; a separate grader evaluates output in its own context window (not influenced by agent reasoning). When something isn't right, the grader pinpoints issues and the agent iterates. Improved task success by up to 10 points, with +8.4% on docx and +10.1% on pptx generation.

**3. Multiagent Orchestration** — A lead agent breaks complex jobs into pieces, delegating to specialist subagents with their own model/prompt/tools. Subagents work in parallel on a shared filesystem. Full traceability in Claude Console.

**Real-world results:**
- **Harvey** (legal): Dreaming → completion rates up ~6x
- **Netflix**: Multiagent orchestration for analyzing logs across hundreds of builds in parallel
- **Spiral by Every**: Lead agent on Haiku delegates drafting to Opus subagents; outcomes enforce editorial quality rubrics

---

**Anthropic 发布 Claude Managed Agents 三大更新：**

1. **Dreaming（研究预览）**：定期回顾Agent会话和记忆，提取模式，整理记忆实现自我改进。能发现重复错误、趋同工作流和团队共享偏好。

2. **Outcomes**：定义成功标准，独立评分器在自己的上下文窗口中评估输出（不受Agent推理影响），未达标则指出问题让Agent重来。任务成功率最高提升10个百分点。

3. **多Agent编排**：主Agent拆解复杂任务，分配给拥有独立模型/提示词/工具的专家子Agent。子Agent共享文件系统并行工作。

**实际效果**：Harvey法律平台完成率提升6倍，Netflix用多Agent并行分析数百个构建日志，Spiral用Outcomes确保写作质量达标。
